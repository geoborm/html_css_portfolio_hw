About Geoff

Geoff Borman was born in New York, New York and grew up in northen New Jersey. He developed a love of theater and film from a young age and went on to major in acting at Boston University. In 2009, having completed his BFA, Geoff moved to back to New York. He lived there until 2020. During this time Geoff worked in a number of positions in theater administration and customer service. Following the COVID 19 pandemic, Geoff made the move to be closer to family, as well as began thinking about a career switch.

Why Programming

While in lockdown, with no comedy, no theater, no customers, Geoff started thinking about a new career path. Enter ZELDA! While playing one of his favorite video games (Zelda), Geoff found himself starting to think, how do they make a game like this? This then brought Geoff to our dear friend Google (link located above). After just a few inquiries, Geoff got his answer: coding! And with his answer Geoff began learning how one goes about developing the skills needed to become a coder or software engineer!
